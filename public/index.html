Welcome to Hack-o-Ween!

Hack-o-Ween will host a series of thirteen horror-themed programming challenges starting in October! Currently, the website is in alpha in order to prepare for the full release. Three puzzles are available, and they can be completed in any order.

These puzzles will not be carried over for the October release.

How to Participate

In order to participate, you first need to log in using GitHub, GitLab, or Google at the top right of the page. Hack-o-Ween works by randomly assigning each user their own unique input to every puzzle. When you're logged in, you'll see that the bottom of every puzzle page will have a link to your program's input. Write a program to solve that input, and submit your solution at the bottom of the puzzle's page.
